Welcome

I'm Megan — a professional dressage rider and coach based in North Wales, working with riders and horses across the UK. I focus on producing horses with care, coaching with clarity, and building partnerships based on trust and feel.

My journey began on a Welsh stud farm, surrounded by native ponies and family tradition. From backing Welsh Section Cs to riding on international youth teams, each chapter has shaped the horsewoman I am today.

Along the way, I’ve worked with everything from sharp ponies to high-level dressage horses, learning to treat each as an individual. Time spent in the Cotswolds deepened my understanding of groundwork, timing, and horsemanship.

Inspired by approaches like those of Tristan Tucker, I’ve developed a calm, consistent method — especially valuable when working with young or anxious horses. My aim is always to create relaxed, confident horses who enjoy their work.

My values are simple: be kind, stay curious, and put the horse first — always.
